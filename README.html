TekaStory

Your intuitive assistant for visual storytelling.

TekaStory is a powerful and intuitive web application designed to streamline your creative process. Visually plan your narrative with customizable panels, title pages, and end cards. Save your projects locally and export them to professional-looking PDFs for easy sharing and presentation.

Core Features

  • Customizable Title & End Pages: Set the stage with a unique title page and conclude your story with a matching end card.
  • Dynamic Storyboard Canvas: Effortlessly add, remove, and reorder storyboard panels with drag-and-drop.
  • Rich Panel Content: Add a visual and a script to each panel. Use [brackets] to highlight parts of your script.
  • Integrated Asset Management: Upload and manage your background images, logos, and panel images directly within the app.
  • Save & Load Projects: Save your entire project, including all assets, into a single .tekastory file. Load it back anytime to continue your work.
  • PDF Export: Generate a clean, professional PDF of your entire storyboard with one click.
  • Undo/Redo History: Work fearlessly with a complete history stack for all your actions.
  • Helpful Tooltips: Get instant guidance on various features directly within the UI.

Getting Started

Getting started with TekaStory is simple. The application opens with a default project, ready for you to customize.

  1. Set Your Project Title: In the "Title & End Page Editor", use the "Project Title (for Saving)" field. This will be used for your filenames.
  2. Customize Your Title Page: Still in the editor, set your on-screen Header and Sub-Header, upload a shared background image, and add a logo for your title and end cards.
  3. Build Your Story: Move down to the "Panels" section. Add images and text to the panels to bring your story to life.
  4. Save Your Work: Click the Save Project button to download a .tekastory file containing your entire project.
  5. Export: When you're ready, click Export to PDF to get a shareable document.

Editing Features

Title & End Page Editor

This is your control center for the opening and closing scenes of your story.

  • Project Title: A dedicated field for your project's filename when saving or exporting.
  • On-Screen Text: Define the Header and Sub-Header text that appears on the title page.
  • Background & Logo: Upload a shared background image and logo for both the title and end pages. You can remove them to revert to the defaults.
  • Logo Placement: Fine-tune the Position and Size of your logo independently for the title and end pages. You can also link their sizes so they change together.

Panels

The canvas is where your narrative takes shape, panel by panel.

  • Adding Panels: Click the Add New Panel button to add more panels to your story.
  • Reordering Panels: Simply click and drag a panel using the grip handle to a new position to reorder your scenes.
  • Panel Content: Upload an image and write your script. Text wrapped in [square brackets] will be highlighted, which is great for actions or camera directions. The script is limited to 6 lines to maintain a clean layout in the final PDF.
  • Removing Panels: Click the trash can icon on a panel to remove it.

Power Features

Project Management

  • Save Project (Ctrl+S): Bundles your entire project—all text, settings, and image assets—into a single .tekastory file for easy storage and sharing.
  • Load Project (Ctrl+O): Open a .tekastory file to load a project back into the editor, exactly as you left it.
  • Pro Tip: A .tekastory file is actually a standard .zip archive. You can rename the file extension to .zip to manually browse and access all your project assets! This is useful for extracting images or backing up specific parts of your project.
